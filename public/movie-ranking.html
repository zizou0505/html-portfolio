The Best Studio Ghibli Movies According to JR

My Top 5 Hayao Miyazaki Movies of All Time

1. Laputa: Castle in the Sky (1986)

This is my favorite Ghibli film. It tackled humanity, nature, technology, and also teen love. This film introduced me to the retrofuturistic style and steampunk genre of the movie industry.

“No matter how many weapons you have,
no matter how great your technology might be,
the world cannot live without love.” -Sheeta

2. Spirited Away (2001)

This movie introduced me to Hayao Miyazaki and Studio Ghibli films. When my cousin was reviewing for her architecture board exam, she stayed in our house for a while. We were kids back then and she would open her laptop playing Ghibli films. This one in particular captured my heart.

"If you completely forget...
You'll never find your way home." - Haku

3. My Neighbor Totoro (1988)

This movie is really cute but somehow nostalgic. Everytime I watch this movie, I think of my family's life back in our countryside province.

"Magnificent tree.
It's been around since long ago,
Back in the time when people and trees used to be friends."
- Tatsuo, To Mei & Satsuki

4. Grave of the Fireflies

This movie is exceptional but I will never watch it again. It broke my heart into pieces. Just writing this paragraph makes my heart ache.

"Why do fireflies have to die so soon?" - Setsuko

5. Howl's Moving Castle

I really liked how imaginative this movie is. Imagine a castle travelling places. Just wow.

"One thing you can always count on is that hearts change." - Prince Justin
